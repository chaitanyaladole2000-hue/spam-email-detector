About Spam Email Detector

Learn how our ML-powered system protects you from spam

What is Spam?

Spam emails are unsolicited messages sent in bulk, often for commercial purposes. They can contain phishing attempts, malware, scams, or unwanted advertisements. Our system helps identify and filter these unwanted emails.

How We Protect You

Our machine learning system analyzes email content and identifies patterns typical of spam. It learns from training data and applies this knowledge to predict new emails accurately.

How Machine Learning Works

1
Training

Model learns from labeled spam and ham email samples

2
Feature Extraction

Converts text to numerical features using TF-IDF

3
Learning Patterns

Model identifies patterns typical of spam emails

4
Prediction

Applies learned knowledge to new emails
